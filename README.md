# ClaimMosaic Documentation

Official documentation for **ClaimMosaic** — the modern claims management software built for public adjusters, independent adjusters, and insurance claims professionals.

## About ClaimMosaic

ClaimMosaic is a comprehensive claims management platform designed to streamline how adjusters handle insurance claims from first notice of loss through final settlement. Whether you're managing property damage claims, tracking policyholder communications, or coordinating with insurance carriers, ClaimMosaic provides the tools you need to run an efficient claims operation.

### Key Features

- **Claims Management** — Track every claim from intake to resolution with detailed claim files, status tracking, and document management
- **Lead Tracking** — Capture and convert leads into active claims with a streamlined pipeline
- **Task Management** — Stay on top of inspections, follow-ups, and deadlines with built-in task workflows
- **Contact & Company Management** — Maintain organized records of policyholders, carriers, contractors, and other stakeholders
- **Mosaic View** — Visualize your workload with calendar, kanban boards, and map views
- **Reporting & Analytics** — Generate reports to analyze claim volume, revenue, and team performance
- **Team Collaboration** — Manage users, permissions, and access rights for your adjusting firm
